General

1. School of Hawking LLP (LLPIN: AAX-9590) (“the Company”) is a registered Limited Liability Partnership that operates Wise Banker (“the Platform”), a digital solution designed to empower individuals in managing their personal finances and discovering suitable financial products.

The primary functionality of the Platform is to help users manage their money effectively through a set of intelligent financial tools. These tools enable users to:

  • . Categorize expenses for better visibility and control over spending,
  • . Set daily spending limits to encourage discipline, and
  • . Define and track goal-based savings plans aligned with their financial objectives.


As a secondary feature, the Platform allows users to explore detailed information about a wide range of financial products such as personal loans, credit cards, and savings accounts. These offerings are presented in collaboration with various licensed NBFCs and banks. Through the app, users can connect directly with the respective sales teams of these institutions for further assistance and application processes.Wise Banker is not a lender or financial institution. It only provides product comparisons and facilitates connections to licensed financial service providers.

Wise Banker does not directly provide or distribute any financial products or services. It acts as a discovery and facilitation platform, bridging the gap between users and financial service providers.


From time to time, the Platform may also introduce additional features to enhance user experience and financial awareness.
